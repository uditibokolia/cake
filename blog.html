Vanilla Cupcakes

Vanilla cupcakes are a classic and popular type of cupcake that feature a delicate vanilla flavor in a moist and tender cake. These cupcakes are typically topped with a creamy frosting, often made with butter and powdered sugar, and can be decorated in a variety of ways.

The base of a vanilla cupcake is typically made with ingredients such as flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, eggs, butter, milk, and vanilla extract. These ingredients are mixed together to create a smooth batter, which is then spooned into cupcake liners and baked until golden brown.

Caramel Bourbon Vanilla Cupcakes

Caramel Bourbon Vanilla Cupcakes are a delicious dessert that combines the flavors of caramel, bourbon, and vanilla in a moist and fluffy cupcake. These cupcakes are perfect for special occasions, parties, or simply as a sweet treat to indulge in.

To make these cupcakes, you'll need to start by making a vanilla cupcake batter with ingredients such as flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, eggs, vanilla extract, and milk. To infuse the cupcakes with the flavors of caramel and bourbon, you'll add caramel sauce and bourbon to the batter. Once the batter is mixed, pour it into cupcake liners and bake until golden brown
